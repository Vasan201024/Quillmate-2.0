Privacy Policy

At Quillmate 2.0, your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name, email, and contact number when you place an order or contact us
  • Order-specific details like service type, delivery method, and uploaded files
  • Payment reference (Transaction ID) for verification

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your writing service orders
  • To respond to customer support inquiries
  • To send order-related communication

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal data with any third-party services or advertisers. Your details are used solely within Quillmate for service fulfillment.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. All submissions are securely sent via Formsphere, and files are uploaded through Dropbox File Request, ensuring end-to-end encryption.

5. Your Consent

By using our website and submitting information through our forms, you consent to our privacy policy.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact Us
